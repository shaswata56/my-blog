The Oracles I have received so far:

* First, there was just awareness in its most primordial form.
* In the physical realm, the energy-space continuum flows through pure awareness.
* What we see is a persistent hallucination and illusion, constructed by the biological body through evolution and adaptation.
* Unknown entities control our lives (and vice versa, recursively).
* From a different reality model, our bedroom is like a centrosome and the kitchen is like mitochondria (just a glimpse of weirdness).
* Birth and death are the processes of awareness's appearance and disappearance through ego within a particular reality model.
* Awareness doesn’t die or get born; it shifts reality models.
* In any instance, for any entity, free will is indistinguishable from fate.
* Various reality models exist in the same time-space.
* Time is a concept; it doesn't really exist.
* Ideologies and cultures are results of other-dimensional entities that affect us. These entities have their own reality models, which I don’t understand.
* Desire emerges from physicality; awareness does not have desire.
* There are entities working to keep everyone ignorant about true reality and to distract them from recognizing our true essence.
